Sarichuks Chicken and Sausage pie Recipe

This Chicken and sausage pie has been in my family for generations and never falls to delight.

Ingredients:




  • 1b boneless, skinless Chicken breast or thights, cut into 1-inch
  • One onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables(e.g., peas, carrots)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water

Instructions:

  1. Make the filling:Cook the chicken, sausage,onion, and garlic in a large skillet until the chicken is cooked through and the sausage is browned.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables, chicken broth, thyme,paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. stir to combine.
  3. Make the crush: ina separate bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold butter and use a pastry blender or your fingers to work it into the flour untgil the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the ice-cold water to the bowl, strring with a fork until the dough comes together in a ball.
  5. Bake the pie for 35-40 minutes, or untill the crust is golden brown and the filling is hot and bubbly.

SERVE WARM AND ENJOY!
